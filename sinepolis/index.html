About Sinéfolis

Sinéfolis Cinemas is Indonesia's premier movie theater chain, offering an unparalleled cinematic experience with state-of-the-art technology and luxurious amenities. With over 50 locations across 10 major cities and more than 200 screens nationwide, we bring you the latest blockbusters and independent films in crystal-clear quality.

Our theaters feature cutting-edge Dolby Atmos sound systems, 4K laser projection, and premium reclining seats that transform every visit into an unforgettable journey.
